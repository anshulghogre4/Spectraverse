# Music Theory: Keys and Emotional Associations

## Major Keys

| Key | Common emotional association | Example use |
|---|---|---|
| C major | Pure, simple, joyful, innocent | Children's songs, hymns, Vivaldi spring |
| G major | Calm, friendly, pastoral, optimistic | Folk music, Beethoven's 6th |
| D major | Triumphant, victorious, bright, ceremonial | Royal fanfares, Pachelbel's Canon |
| A major | Confident, declarative, bright, hopeful | Mozart Clarinet Concerto |
| E major | Brilliant, sparkling, energetic | Bach Partita No. 3, Beethoven 1st piano concerto |
| F major | Calm, complacent, tender, peaceful | Beethoven 6th, hymns |
| B♭ major | Cheerful, hopeful, generous | Mozart, Strauss waltzes |
| E♭ major | Heroic, bold, expansive | Beethoven Eroica, Strauss Hero's Life |

## Minor Keys

| Key | Common emotional association | Example use |
|---|---|---|
| A minor | Tender melancholy, lyrical sadness | Bach English Suite, Albinoni Adagio |
| E minor | Restless yearning, plaintive | Brahms 4th symphony |
| D minor | Tragic, dark, serious, "doom" | Mozart Requiem, Bach Toccata & Fugue, Wagner Flying Dutchman |
| G minor | Anxious, dramatic, agitated | Mozart 40th symphony |
| C minor | Tragic-heroic, struggle, fate | Beethoven 5th symphony |
| F minor | Despair, depression, funereal | Beethoven Appassionata |
| B♭ minor | Gloomy, oppressive, dark | Tchaikovsky 1st piano concerto opening |
| F♯ minor | Resentment, discontent, passionate | Bach French Suite No. 6 |

## Modes (church modes, modal jazz)

- **Dorian** (minor scale with raised 6th): jazzy, mysterious, "cool" — Miles Davis "So What"
- **Phrygian** (minor scale with lowered 2nd): Spanish, exotic, dark — flamenco, metal
- **Lydian** (major scale with raised 4th): dreamy, otherworldly, magical — Simpsons theme
- **Mixolydian** (major scale with lowered 7th): bluesy, folk, Celtic — bagpipe music
- **Locrian** (minor with lowered 2nd and 5th): unstable, dissonant — rare, Björk

## Image-to-Key Mapping Rules (synesthetic)

Use the following deterministic rules when no other guidance exists:

- **Bright + warm image (sunny, golden, orange, yellow)** → C major or D major
- **Bright + cool image (sky, ocean, daylight)** → A major or E major
- **Dark + warm image (sunset, fire, candlelight)** → D minor or A minor
- **Dark + cool image (night, storm, deep ocean)** → C minor or F minor
- **High-energy chaotic image (fireworks, crowd, lightning)** → E major (high energy) or G minor (anxious energy)
- **Calm pastoral image (meadow, lake)** → F major or G major
- **Mysterious / surreal image** → Dorian or Lydian mode
- **Spiritual / sacred image (temple, cathedral)** → Lydian, or A minor with drone
